# Minimal Brutalist Mobile Design System

## 1. Design Principles

* Raw, minimal, and unapologetically functional
* No decoration, no softness
* Strong contrast and rigid structure
* Typography and layout over visual styling
* Eliminate all non-essential elements
* Designed for clarity and speed, not comfort

---

## 2. Color System

### 2.1 Light Mode

* Primary: #000000
* Background: #FFFFFF
* Surface: #FFFFFF
* Text Primary: #000000
* Text Secondary: #525252
* Border: #000000
* Divider: #D4D4D4

### 2.2 Dark Mode

* Primary: #FFFFFF
* Background: #000000
* Surface: #000000
* Text Primary: #FFFFFF
* Text Secondary: #A3A3A3
* Border: #FFFFFF

### 2.3 Rules

* Strict black & white base
* No gradients, no soft colors
* Color used only when absolutely necessary (alerts)
* High contrast is mandatory

---

## 3. Typography

* Font Family:

  * iOS: System
  * Android: Roboto
  * Optional: monospace or grotesk font

### Scale

* Large Title: 26 / Bold
* Title: 20 / Bold
* Body: 15 / Regular
* Caption: 12 / Regular
* Small: 10 / Medium

### Rules

* Typography is the primary visual tool
* Minimal font variation
* Line height: 1.3 – 1.5
* Use spacing and weight, not color

---

## 4. Spacing System

Base unit: 4

* xs: 4
* sm: 8
* md: 12
* lg: 16
* xl: 20

### Rules

* Tight and controlled spacing
* No excessive whitespace
* Align everything strictly

---

## 5. Layout & Safe Area

* Respect safe area
* Use rigid grid alignment
* Full-width layouts preferred
* Avoid floating elements

---

## 6. Touch & Interaction

* Minimum touch target:

  * iOS: 44pt
  * Android: 48dp

* Feedback:

  * Instant opacity change
  * No animation or scaling

* Interactions should feel immediate

---

## 7. Navigation Patterns

* Stack-based navigation

### Behavior

* Simple headers
* No decorative navigation
* Clear hierarchy

---

## 8. Components

### 8.1 Button

Variants:

* Primary (filled black/white)
* Secondary (outline)

Rules:

* Height: 40–44
* Radius: 0 (no rounding)
* Strong border
* No shadows, no gradients

---

### 8.2 Card

* Optional, often avoided
* If used:

  * Border: 1–2px
  * Radius: 0
  * No shadow

---

### 8.3 List Item

* Height: 48–60
* Strong divider lines
* Left-aligned text
* No unnecessary icons

---

### 8.4 Input

* Border: 1–2px solid
* Radius: 0
* Background: transparent or white
* Focus: strong border highlight

---

## 9. Motion

* Duration: 0–100ms

### Style

* Almost no animation
* Instant transitions
* No easing or bounce

---

## 10. Elevation & Depth

* No shadows
* No layering effects
* Depth created only by layout

---

## 11. Iconography

* Minimal or none

* If used:

  * Simple line icons
  * No decoration

* Sizes:

  * Small: 14
  * Default: 16–18
  * Large: 20

---

## 12. Accessibility

* Extremely high contrast
* Clear visual boundaries
* Avoid relying on subtle cues

---

## 13. Platform Adaptation

### iOS

* Strip down default styling
* Avoid excessive system polish

### Android

* Override Material softness
* Keep rigid layout

---

## 14. Do / Don't

### Do

* Keep everything simple and direct
* Use strong contrast
* Align everything precisely

### Don't

* Add decoration
* Use rounded corners
* Use shadows or gradients
* Introduce unnecessary visual elements

---
